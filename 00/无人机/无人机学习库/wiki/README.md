# Wiki

This is the synthesized knowledge layer.

Use these folders:

- `sources/`: one note per important raw source.
- `concepts/`: reusable concepts and principles.
- `entities/`: companies, organizations, teams, people.
- `systems/`: technical systems and architectures.
- `products/`: product or equipment notes.
- `projects/`: active project dossiers.
- `requirements/`: requirements, constraints, test points.
- `standards/`: regulations, standards, certification notes.
- `comparisons/`: competitor, option, and tradeoff comparisons.
- `decisions/`: decisions and rationale.
- `questions/`: open questions and research tasks.
- `glossary/`: terms, abbreviations, definitions.
