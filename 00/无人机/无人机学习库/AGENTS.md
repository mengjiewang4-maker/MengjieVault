# Repository Instructions

This vault follows an LLM Wiki workflow inspired by Andrej Karpathy's public notes:

- Treat `raw/` as append-only source material. Do not rewrite, summarize in place, or rename original files unless the user explicitly asks.
- Write synthesized knowledge into `wiki/`.
- Every non-trivial wiki note should cite its source files under `raw/`.
- Keep `wiki/index.md` as the navigation entry point.
- Keep `wiki/log.md` as the chronological record of major ingest, synthesis, and restructuring work.
- Use `templates/` when creating new notes.
- Put user-facing deliverables in `outputs/`, not in `wiki/`.

Preferred note style:

- Use clear Chinese headings and concise paragraphs.
- Use Obsidian wikilinks for internal concepts, projects, systems, and standards.
- Keep uncertainty explicit with sections such as "待确认", "证据", and "下一步".
- Do not mix raw excerpts and synthesized conclusions without labeling them.
