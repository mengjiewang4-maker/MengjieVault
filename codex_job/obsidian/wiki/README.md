# Wiki

This layer stores structured knowledge after raw material has been processed.
这一层存放原始材料整理后的结构化知识。

- `system/`: operating notes, templates, workflows, and navigation
  `system/`：运行说明、模板、工作流和导航页面
- `research/`: active research topics, models, simulations, projects, and experiments
  `research/`：活跃研究主题、模型、仿真、项目和实验
- `papers/`: inbox, paper notes, reviews, and reproduction notes
  `papers/`：文献收件箱、论文笔记、综述和复现记录
- `knowledge/`: stable domain knowledge in physics, math, and engineering
  `knowledge/`：物理、数学和工程等相对稳定的领域知识
- `learning/`: skill-building notes for Python and quant work
  `learning/`：Python 和量化等技能学习笔记
