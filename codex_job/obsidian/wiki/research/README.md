# Research

Research pages track active scientific and technical work.
研究页面用于跟踪正在进行的科学和技术工作。

- `topics/`: concept clusters, surveys, comparisons, and topic notes
  `topics/`：概念簇、综述、比较和专题笔记
- `models/`: cross-domain mappings and formal models
  `models/`：跨领域映射和形式化模型
- `simulations/`: simulation methods and numerical workflows
  `simulations/`：仿真方法和数值工作流
- `projects/`: project pages and project MOCs
  `projects/`：项目页面和项目 MOC
- `experiments/`: experimental design, execution, and validation notes
  `experiments/`：实验设计、执行和验证笔记
